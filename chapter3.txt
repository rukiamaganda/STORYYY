Chapter 3: The River of Memories

Kneeling by the river, Mycah gazed into the water. Faces and moments from her childhood flickered across the surface—her parents, lost to a mysterious illness, her first steps, her grandmother’s stories. The river called to her, promising answers. With trembling hands, she touched the water.

A surge of visions overwhelmed her. She saw a hidden temple deep within the mountains, a masked figure watching her, and a key shaped like a crescent moon. Gasping, Mycah pulled back, the locket glowing brightly. She realized the journey was not just about her, but about a power that could save or doom the world. Renewed with purpose, she followed the river’s bend, searching for the path to the mountains.
